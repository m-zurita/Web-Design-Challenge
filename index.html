Summary: Latitude vs X.

This project is about weather changes according to Earth’s position. To accomplish the analysis, cities all over the world were taken into consideration (Source: OpenWeatherMaps).

By using MatPlotLib, we are able to visually recognize the effects of latitude on weather, via four variables: temperature, cloudiness, wind speed, and humidity. For more information you may click on each graph or dropdown menu above.
